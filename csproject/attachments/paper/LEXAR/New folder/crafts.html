When you get very stressed you might have felt to engage your your focus on something else just to divert to your minds. Well DIY is a good solution to it for creative and want-to-become-creative people. Crafts not only engane your your mind but also helps you to become more creative. This will also help you in your work life to give more ideas to your boss. Sounds amazing right?

We will give you some ideas. Try it out and become creative :)

1. Handmade Diary

    • Get the materials (which you'll probably find at home) and get started. You'll need a pencil too, for the basic structure of your diary. Read the "Tips" section before you begin working on the diary.
      Join all the normal sheets and compact them with your hand.Spread a thick layer of glue on the side of the compressed sheets you think the spine of the diary will be.
      Now that the glue in the spine of the book is dry, cut a straight piece of paper, and glue it to the glued spine of the book. Take the cardboard paper and with the white/liquid glue, glue firmly each piece of cardboard to the first and last normal sheet pages of the notebook.
      Reinforce the spine of the book with the half piece of cardboard.
      Start to decorate the cover of your diary!

    • It's done

    2. Good Things' Jar

    • Gather the materials required- A glass jar, paper pieces(7cm*4cm), thread.
      Decorate the jar as per your need. You can use glitters, color sheets, stickers with designs etc to decorate the jar.
      The main things which are needed to make this Good things' jar are some good things you did. If you haven't do it. But are pretty sure that you might have done something good in your life atleast once.
      Write down the good things you did in the piese of paper which you have prepared initially.
        Roll those paper pieces and tie it with a piece of thread and put it in the jar

      Your 'Good things' jar' is ready
